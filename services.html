Our Comprehensive Services

From property discovery to post-purchase support, Compass Housing Society provides end-to-end real estate solutions designed for modern homebuyers and investors.

Personalized Property Consultation

One-on-one sessions with our real estate experts to understand your needs, budget, and preferences.

  • Needs assessment & budget
  • Location analysis & shortlisting
  • Comparative market evaluation
  • Customized property

Guided Site Visits Consultation

Organized visits to ongoing and completed projects with detailed walkthroughs and expert insights.

  • Virtual & physical tour
  • Project developer meetings
  • Construction quality inspection
  • Neighborhood exploration

Legal & Documentation Assistance

Comprehensive support for all legal formalities, verification, and registration processes.

  • Title deed verification
  • RERA compliance checks
  • Loan documentation support
  • Registration & stamp duty

Home Loan Facilitation Consultation

Partnership with leading banks and financial institutions for seamless loan processing.

  • Pre-approval assistance
  • Interest rate comparison
  • Documentation preparation
  • EMI planning & optimization

Interior Design & Customization

End-to-end interior solutions to personalize your space according to your lifestyle.

  • Space planning & 3D
  • Material selection & sourcing
  • Modular kitchen & wardrobe
  • Turnkey execution & project

Post-Purchase Property Management

Ongoing support for maintenance, rentals, and community living after you move in.

  • Maintenance coordination
  • Rental management services
  • Society formation guidance
  • Renewal & upgrade assistance
